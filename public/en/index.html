FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do you offer online consultations?
Yes. You can book either a video consultation or an in-office meeting. The same level of rigour and confidentiality applies to both formats.
What happens after booking?
You receive immediate confirmation with all the details needed to prepare the consultation. A concise summary of your situation and key documents help optimise the session.
Can I reschedule after confirmation?
Yes. Reply to the confirmation email to request a new time slot as soon as possible.
What types of matters does the office handle?
The office primarily works in family and patrimonial law (divorce, succession, matrimonial regimes, gifts) and labor law (dismissal, negotiated termination, litigation) — for both individuals and companies.
How quickly can I get a first appointment?
Available slots are shown directly on the website. A first response is generally provided within 24 hours.
