Privacy Policy

This policy describes how personal data submitted through the booking and contact forms on this website is processed.

Purpose

Data submitted is used only to process your request and provide follow-up on that request.

Legal basis

Processing is based on the legitimate interest of the office to organise pre-contractual exchanges and respond to incoming enquiries.

Retention period

Form data is retained for 6 months after request processing.

Cookies

Optional cookies are activated only after explicit opt-in consent. No optional cookie is set by default.

Your rights

You may request access, rectification, deletion or restriction of processing of your personal data by contacting the office at the details listed in the Legal Notice.

If you are unsatisfied with how your data is handled, you may lodge a complaint with the CNIL (Commission Nationale de l'Informatique et des Libertés).
